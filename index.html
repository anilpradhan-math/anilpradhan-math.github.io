About

I am a cryptography researcher with over a decade of experience bridging theoretical mathematics and production-grade cryptographic solutions. My primary focus is Fully Homomorphic Encryption — specifically novel CRT-based and Multi-Key FHE schemes — alongside encrypted data sharing, secure multi-party computation, zero-knowledge proofs, and post-quantum protocol design.


Currently leading cryptography research at Vaultree in Dublin, where I direct the engineering of a proprietary FHE library in Rust and Python, and represent the company at major cybersecurity and privacy conferences worldwide.
